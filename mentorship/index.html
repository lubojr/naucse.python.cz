Mentorship programme

About the programme

With finishing a Beginners Python Course your coding journey does not stop! On the contrary actually. Now you are familiar with building blocks and it is on you to decide in which directions to go. There is no limit. And mentors and our mentorship programme can help you. This programme is aiming on connecting mentees with mentors based on needs and wishes of the mentee and expertise of the mentors. It would be great if you are able to define what you are looking for, but if you just want a learning buddy it is also fine.

For whom is the mentoring programme?

  • You are looking for someone with specific knowledge in a field who can help you achieve your goals.
  • You want to continue studying and want a buddy who will hold you accountable and help with tips and tricks when needed.
  • You want to land a job in IT and want someone to help you on this journey.
  • You want to jump from junior to medior and build a skills (not only technical) needed.

How to join as a mentee and how does it work?

  1. As a mentee, fill out the google form https://forms.gle/eZWixWefMgrbpbFx6 with your details and what you are interested in.
  2. We will reach out to you once we find an available mentor matching your wishes - it can take some time, but we shall not forget about you!
  3. We will put you in touch with your mentor and provide you with a starting guide and draft possible first steps.

How to join as a mentor?

  1. As a mentor, fill out the google form https://forms.gle/2EGoUeSri5cAaPbh6 with your details and what you are interested in.
  2. We will reach out to you once we select an available mentee matching your expertise - it can take some time, but we shall not forget about you!
  3. We will put you in touch with your mentee and provide you with a starting guide and draft possible first steps.

Other information.

The programme is not formalized at this point - you can agree on video calls, personal meetings or email communication as you prefer and same goes for the frequency. Please keep in mind that all of us are doing this in our free time and mentoring doesn't mean that a mentor is available all the time. The planned duration of the mentorship will be defined by the mentor and mentee upon shared expectation and understanding. However, we expect the average duration of the programme to be around 6 months.

This program is free of charge.
